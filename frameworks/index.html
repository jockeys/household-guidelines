Tier 1: Proactive Foundation

Goal: Build a strong family culture and solve problems before they start.

Use: Positive Discipline

Employ tools like the weekly Family Meeting to give everyone a voice, co-create rules, and foster a sense of belonging and significance.

Tier 2: Reactive Containment

Goal: Immediately and calmly stop a known "Stop" behavior (whining, arguing).

Use: 1-2-3 Magic

Apply the calm, non-emotional 1-2-3 counting method to re-establish a boundary without getting into a power struggle.

Tier 3: Teachable Moments

Goal: Turn a mistake into a lesson in responsibility.

Use: Love & Logic

When a child makes an "affordable mistake," respond with empathy and allow them to experience the logical consequence of their choice.
