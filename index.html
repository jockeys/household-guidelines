Our Parenting Toolkit

We've adopted principles from three evidence-based frameworks to guide us. This gives us a shared playbook for responding to challenges consistently and effectively.

🔢

1-2-3 Magic

For clear, non-emotional discipline. When a "Stop" behavior occurs, we calmly count. This reduces arguing and helps us stay consistent.

🤝

Positive Discipline

To be "kind but firm." We focus on long-term solutions, mutual respect, and involving children in problem-solving.

💡

Love & Logic

To empower kids through empathy and logical consequences. This teaches responsibility by linking choices to outcomes.

🌙 Evening Routine: Calm & Consistent

Our wind-down sequence starts at 7:00 PM to ensure everyone is settled for an 8:00 PM bedtime. Predictability helps reduce resistance and improve sleep.

🍽️

7:00 PM - Clean-Up

All kids help clear their dishes. Screens turn off for the night.

🚿

7:15 PM - Hygiene Time

Showers/baths and brushing teeth.

📖

7:45 PM - Wind-Down

Quiet time with books or puzzles. No screens!

😴

8:00 PM - Lights Out

Tuck-ins, cuddles, and goodnight wishes.

🍔 Mealtime Mantras

  • 🧘

    No Pressure to Eat

    We provide healthy food; kids decide how much. No force-feeding.

  • 🚫

    Screen-Free Zone

    This rule is for everyone, adults included, to encourage family connection.

  • ✨

    Clean Up Your Space

    Everyone is responsible for clearing their own plate from the table.

🧸 Toys & Property Rules

  • 🔒

    Respect Personal Items

    Special toys don't have to be shared. Always ask permission first.

  • 🔄

    Practice Turn-Taking

    Toys in common areas are for everyone. We use timers if needed.

  • 👶

    The Toddler "Trade"

    To interact with our 18-month-old, older kids can offer to trade a toy instead of taking one.

Our Commitment as Partners

🤫

Private Dialogue

We resolve disagreements away from the children to maintain a secure environment and our authority as a team.

We always back each other up. We use "we" language to show the children we are in this together, always.
