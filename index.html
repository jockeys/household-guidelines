The Integrated OS ‚Üí

Our parenting strategy is a multi-layered system that provides the right tool for the right situation. This is the core of our Parenting Engine.

Foundation Layer

Nonviolent Communication (NVC) is how we talk to each other to build connection.

Proactive Layer

Collaborative & Proactive Solutions (CPS) is how we solve recurring problems together.

Daily & Relational Layer

Love & Logic and Positive Discipline are how we manage daily life and build skills.

Emergency Layer

1-2-3 Magic is how we stop immediate safety risks. Used sparingly.
